Leader Board FAQ

What is the Leader Board?

  • The Leaderboard is a combined list of all the pilots on the server, it is updated roughtly every 4 to 8 hours.
  • Users are scored based on a number of items and then Ranked.
  • You can pull your personal logobook by clicking on your name


SCORING

  • You gain and loose points based on a number of items.

Gaining Points.

  • Buildings: 1 Point
  • PVP Kill: 1 Point
  • Air to Ground Kill: 1 Point
  • Air to Helicopter Kill: 2 Points
  • Air to Air Kill: 5 Points
  • Air to Sea Kill: 10 Point

Loosing Points.

  • PVP Loss: 1 Point
  • Pilot Death UH1 Gunner: 5 Point
  • Pilot Death Helicopter: 10 Points
  • Pilot Death Mud Movers: 15 Points
  • RIO Death: 15 Points
  • Pilot Death All Others: 25 Points
  • Destruction/Crash Helicopter,: 10 Points
  • Destruction/Crash Helicopter as Gunner: 5 Points
  • Destruction/Crash Mud Movers: 15 Points
  • Destruction/Crash RIO: 15 Points
  • Destruction/Crash All Others: 20 Points
  • Ejection Helicopter: 5 Points
  • Ejection Helicopter Gunnder: 5 Points
  • Ejection All Others: 10 Points
