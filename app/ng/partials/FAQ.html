Operation Persia Aflame - GCI Information

The Operation Persia Aflame, web GCI has been developed by the TGW team with the aim of providing the DCS community a portal to:

  • Plan Missions
  • View and monitor the operations progress
  • Monitor their achievements and flight hours within our comprohensive pilot logbook/leaderboard


Important Information

  • The intel found within the GCI map is randomly generated every 5 mins.
  • The intel does not represent the entire battle space. Only information that has been reported by the coalition intelligence agency.
  • Strike mission markers are (to the best of intelligence officers skills) accurate
  • SEAD, and Scud hunting mission markers are only a general reference the actual unit is located within a 20km range.
